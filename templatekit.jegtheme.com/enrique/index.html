Voice Assistant for Music

The Voice Assistant for Music is a Python-based application that enables users to control music playback on YouTube using voice commands. It allows users to play, pause, skip, or search for specific songs directly on YouTube, making it easy to enjoy hands-free music. Utilizing libraries like SpeechRecognition and webbrowser, the assistant enhances the user experience by providing seamless music control through voice, making it a convenient tool for music lovers. Perfect for those who want quick access to YouTube's vast music library with just a voice command.
